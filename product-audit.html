1. Executive Summary

ProHome Sales operatsion chuqurlik bo'yicha kuchli, lekin product polish, orientation va commercial readiness bo'yicha notekis. Tizim ichida foydali modullar bor, ammo foydalanuvchi birinchi kirganda premium va to'liq mahsulot hissi har doim ham shakllanmaydi.

  • Operatsion chuqurlik bor, lekin entry-point sifati past.
  • TJM va contract flow kuchli, CRM esa semantik va strukturaviy jihatdan zaifroq.
  • Dashboard zamonaviy ko'rinadi, lekin actionability yetarli emas.
  • UI komponentlari yaxshi, lekin product orchestration notekis.
  • Production ichidagi unfinished signallar trustni pasaytiradi.

Business impactTrust, onboarding va adoption tezligi tushadi.

3. Hozirning O'zida Kuchli Joylar

  • TJM flow kuchli: filter, block select, status legend, statistik summary va URL state sync mavjud.
  • Room details ichida calculator va contract generation mavjud, bu real business value beradi.
  • Contracts moduli pishiq: project switch, search, status tabs, stats va file download flow mavjud.
  • Dashboard vizual tili tartibli va professional ko'rinadi.

4. Bizda Yo'q Narsalar

  • Role-based real home dashboard.
  • Global command center: project switch, universal search, notifications va quick actions.
  • Full CRM fields: source, owner, reminder, note, next step, last interaction.
  • Lead to inventory link va aniq unit binding.
  • Decision support: alerts, anomaly signal va recommended actions.
  • Saved filters va views.
  • Audit trail va accountability layer.

6. Yechimlar

Yechim 1: Root home'ni role-based command center qilish

Top metrics, pending tasks, latest contracts, hot leads va quick actions bloklarini root route'da yig'ish.

Impact: high | Effort: medium | Priority: P1

Yechim 2: Settings placeholder'larini tozalash

Placeholderni olib tashlash yoki to'liq yakunlash, kerak bo'lsa feature flag bilan yashirish.

Impact: high | Effort: low | Priority: P1

Yechim 3: CRM schema'ni normalizatsiya qilish

Fieldlarni aniq phone, client_name, company, source, note maydonlariga ajratish va backend mapping contractini tozalash.

Impact: high | Effort: high | Priority: P1

Yechim 4: CRM follow-up engine qo'shish

Reminder, task, owner, next action va SLA qo'shish; lead drawer va dashboard bilan ulash.

Impact: high | Effort: medium | Priority: P1

Yechim 5: Global navigation'ni chuqurlashtirish

Universal search, project switch va quick actions bilan shell UX'ni control center darajasiga olib chiqish.

Impact: medium | Effort: medium | Priority: P2

Yechim 6: Dashboard'ni action-oriented qilish

Alerts, compare mode, drilldown va recommended next steps qo'shish.

Impact: high | Effort: medium | Priority: P1

Yechim 7: Modullar integratsiyasini kuchaytirish

Lead -> unit -> contract lifecycle ni bitta data storyga aylantirish.

Impact: high | Effort: high | Priority: P1

Yechim 8: Performance optimizatsiyasi

Qo'shimcha code-splitting va visibility-based lazy loading bilan heavy route'larni yengillashtirish.

Impact: medium | Effort: medium | Priority: P2
